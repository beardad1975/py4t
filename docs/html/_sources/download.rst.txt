thonnyPy4t 安裝檔(for windows)
================================



py4t v0.3 (2020-11-10建立) 

`下載連結(安裝檔) <https://github.com/beardad1975/py4t/releases/download/v0.3/thonnyPy4t-0.3.exe>`_

註：安裝時若有出現Defender SmartScreen的通知訊息，可點選 [ **其他資訊** ]，[ **仍要執行** ]，來進行安裝

----------------------

`下載連結(免安裝檔) <https://github.com/beardad1975/py4t/releases/download/v0.3/thonnyPy4t-0.3-windows-portable.zip>`_

註：解壓縮後，執行資料夾內的thonny.exe
